Anup Parekh

I create products that transform complexity into simplicity. Loyalty programmes at Amex, tax compliance at Avalara, and now a wearable for grassroots footballers.

I've also spent 12 years as a foster carer, mostly for older teenagers and asylum-seeking young people. It's shaped how I work: I'm good with ambiguity, I don't give up easily, and I've learned that the best solutions start with listening.

I'm most useful when something's messy and needs to actually ship.
